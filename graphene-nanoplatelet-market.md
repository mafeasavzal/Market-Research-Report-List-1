<p><p>The graphene nanoplatelet market can be categorized into two main types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic graphene nanoplatelets. Hydrophobic graphene nanoplatelets are characterized by their resistance to water, making them suitable for applications in coatings and materials that require moisture resistance. In contrast, hydrophilic graphene nanoplatelets have an affinity for water, enhancing their dispersibility in aqueous solutions, which is beneficial for applications in biomedical and environmental sectors. Both types are crucial for advancing nanotechnology and various industrial applications.</p></p>
