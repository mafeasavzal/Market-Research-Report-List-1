<p><p>The Diagnostic X-Ray System market consists of three primary types. Analog X-Ray Systems utilize film-based technology for imaging, offering a traditional method of capturing diagnostic images but are less favored due to longer processing times. Digital X-Ray Systems have gained popularity as they provide immediate imaging, enhanced clarity, and easier storage and sharing capabilities. Retrofit X-Ray Systems involve upgrading existing analog equipment to digital technology, offering cost-effective solutions for healthcare facilities seeking modern capabilities without full equipment replacement.</p></p>
