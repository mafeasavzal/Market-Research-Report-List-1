<p><strong>The Surge Protection Devices (SPD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch SPD</li><li>Voltage Limiting Type SPD</li><li>Combination Type SPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surge Protection Devices (SPDs) are crucial for safeguarding electrical systems from voltage spikes. The Voltage Switch SPD disconnects the circuit during a surge, preventing damage. In contrast, the Voltage Limiting Type SPD clamps the voltage to a safe level, allowing normal operation without interruption. The Combination Type SPD combines both functionalities, offering a comprehensive solution by disconnecting during severe surges and limiting voltage for smaller spikes. These advanced SPDs are essential in various applications to ensure reliable and safe electrical performance.</p></p>
