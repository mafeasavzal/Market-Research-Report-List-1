<p><ul><li>DIP Type</li><li>SMD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Xtal market types refer to the different packaging formats for crystal oscillators used in electronic circuits. The DIP (Dual In-line Package) type features dual rows of pins for through-hole mounting, making them suitable for prototyping and applications requiring manual insertion. Conversely, the SMD (Surface-Mount Device) type is designed for surface-mount technology, allowing for compact designs and automated assembly. Both types serve various applications, with DIP often favored for accessibility and SMD for efficiency in modern electronics.</p></p>
