<p><p>Web Content Management Software (WCMS) can be categorized primarily into two market types: Cloud-Based and Web-Based solutions. In contrast, Web-Based WCMS is accessed through web browsers and typically requires minimal setup. It allows for straightforward content creation and editing without installing additional software, making it user-friendly for organizations of all sizes.</p></p>
