<p><p>The Extended Body Gate Valves market features two primary types: Screw Type and Socket Weld Type. Screw Type valves are designed for manual operation, allowing precise control through a threaded stem, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, Socket Weld Type valves offer a seamless connection through welding, ensuring lower leak potential and better structural integrity. Both types are vital in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, providing reliable flow control solutions tailored to specific piping needs.</p></p>
