<p><ul><li>Belt Juicer</li><li>Cup Juicer</li><li>Air Bag Juicer</li><li>Spiral Juicer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial juicer equipment market consists of various types catering to different juice extraction needs. Belt juicers utilize a belt-driven system for efficient juice extraction, while cup juicers collect juice in designated containers for easy access. Air bag juicers leverage air pressure to extract juice from fruits and vegetables, offering a gentle process. Spiral juicers, known for their masticating method, effectively extract juice by slowly grinding produce. Other types may include hydraulic and centrifugal juicers, addressing diverse operational requirements across industries.</p></p>
