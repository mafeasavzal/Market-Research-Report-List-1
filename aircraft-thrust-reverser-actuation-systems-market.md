<p><p>Aircraft thrust reverser actuation systems play a crucial role in decelerating aircraft during landing by redirecting engine thrust. The market is primarily divided into hydraulic and electric systems. Hydraulic systems utilize pressurized fluid to engage thrust reversers, offering high force and reliability. Conversely, electric systems leverage electric motors, providing more precise control and reducing weight and maintenance needs. Both systems are vital for enhancing aircraft performance and safety, catering to different operational requirements and preferences in modern aviation.</p></p>
