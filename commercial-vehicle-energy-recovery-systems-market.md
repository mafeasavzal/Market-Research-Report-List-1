<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Energy Recovery Systems market comprises two key types: the Automotive Regenerative Braking System and the Automotive Waste Heat Recovery System. Regenerative braking systems capture and store energy typically lost during braking, converting it into electrical power to enhance fuel efficiency. In contrast, waste heat recovery systems utilize excess heat generated by engine operation to power auxiliary systems or improve overall vehicle efficiency. Both technologies contribute to reducing fuel consumption and emissions, promoting sustainable transportation solutions.</p></p>
