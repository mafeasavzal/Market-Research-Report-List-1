<p><ul><li>Manual Pellet Boilers</li><li>Semi-automatic Pellet Boilers</li><li>Fully-automatic Pellet Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pellet boiler system market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic pellet boilers. Manual pellet boilers require users to manually feed pellets and manage operations, offering lower initial costs but higher labor input. Semi-automatic boilers automate some processes, reducing user intervention while still needing manual pellet loading. Fully-automatic pellet boilers provide the highest level of convenience, automatically handling fuel feeding, combustion, and temperature control, making them ideal for users seeking efficiency and minimal maintenance.</p></p>
