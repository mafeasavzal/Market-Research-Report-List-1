<p><p>Ultrapure water purification systems are essential for industries requiring high-purity water, such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. The market comprises several technologies: </p><p>1. **Reverse Osmosis (RO)** removes contaminants through a semi-permeable membrane, ensuring high purity. </p><p>2. **Electrodeionization (EDI)** combines ion exchange and electric current, eliminating ions and impurities effectively. </p><p>3. **Ion Exchange** uses resin to replace undesirable ions with less harmful ones, offering a versatile solution for specific contaminant removal. Each technology plays a crucial role in meeting stringent water quality standards.</p></p>
