<p><p>The hypodermic safety needles market is categorized into two main types: active safety needles and passive safety needles. Active safety needles require the user to engage a protective mechanism after needle use, offering a proactive approach to prevent needle-stick injuries. In contrast, passive safety needles feature automatic mechanisms that shield the needle immediately after use, reducing the risk of exposure without user intervention. Both types aim to enhance safety for healthcare professionals and minimize the potential for infections.</p></p>
