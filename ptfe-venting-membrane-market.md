<p><p>The PTFE venting membrane market comprises two main types: adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents feature a sticky side that allows for easy application and secure attachment to surfaces, ensuring effective venting and protection against contaminants. Vents without backing material are designed to be integrated into systems where adhesion is not required, offering flexibility in various applications. Both types enhance performance by allowing trapped gases or vapors to escape while preventing liquid ingress.</p></p>
