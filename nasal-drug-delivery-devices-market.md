<p><ul><li>Multidose</li><li>Uni/bidose</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nasal drug delivery devices are categorized into multidose and uni/bidose formats. Multidose devices allow multiple applications from a single container, enhancing convenience and patient compliance for chronic conditions. They typically use a mechanical or electronic pump for consistent dosing. On the other hand, uni/bidose systems deliver a single or two doses per use, offering precision and minimizing contamination risk. These formats cater to varying patient needs, ensuring effective medication delivery through the nasal route.</p></p>
