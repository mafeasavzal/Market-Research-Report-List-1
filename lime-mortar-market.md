<p><strong>The Lime Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Lime Mortars</li><li>Non-hydraulic Lime Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lime mortar market encompasses two primary types: hydraulic lime mortars and non-hydraulic lime mortars. Hydraulic lime mortars contain hydraulic components that allow them to set and harden in both wet and dry conditions, making them ideal for structures exposed to moisture. In contrast, non-hydraulic lime mortars rely on carbonatation for curing, setting only in dry conditions and offering greater flexibility and breathability. Both types are valued for their sustainability, versatility, and compatibility with historical masonry restoration projects.</p></p>
