<p><p>The semiconductor packaging used solder paste market comprises two main types: leaded solder paste and lead-free solder paste. Leaded solder paste contains tin and lead, offering excellent reliability for traditional applications, but faces environmental regulations due to lead toxicity. In contrast, lead-free solder paste replaces lead with alternative materials, such as silver or copper, aligning with eco-friendly standards and regulations. The shift towards lead-free solutions is driven by industry demands for safer, sustainable manufacturing practices without compromising performance.</p></p>
