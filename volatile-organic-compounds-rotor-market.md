<p><p>The Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) Rotor market primarily involves two types of materials: zeolite and activated carbon. Activated carbon, derived from carbon-rich materials, is highly porous and excels in capturing a wide range of organic compounds, providing high adsorption efficiency. Both materials are crucial in enhancing air quality and reducing pollution in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
