<p><p>The fish powder market is primarily divided into two types: Steam Dried (SD) Fish Powder and Flame Dried (FD) Fish Powder. SD fish powder is produced using steam heat, which preserves essential nutrients and offers a finer texture, making it suitable for various dietary applications. In contrast, FD fish powder is made using direct flame, resulting in a stronger flavor and slightly coarser texture, often preferred in traditional cuisines. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs across food and supplement industries.</p></p>
