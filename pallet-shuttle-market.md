<p><p>The Pallet Shuttle market includes two primary types: Standard Pallet Shuttle and Four Way Pallet Shuttle. The Standard Pallet Shuttle operates on a traditional lane system, allowing for efficient storage and retrieval of pallets in a dense racking setup. In contrast, the Four Way Pallet Shuttle provides greater maneuverability, enabling pallets to be accessed from four directions, which enhances flexibility in warehouse layouts. Both types optimize space utilization and streamline logistics processes, catering to different operational needs in warehousing.</p></p>
