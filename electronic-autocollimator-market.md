<p><ul><li>Without Micrometers</li><li>With Micrometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic autocollimator market consists of two primary types: those without micrometers and those with micrometers. Autocollimators without micrometers utilize electronic sensors for precise angular measurements, making them suitable for general applications where high accuracy is needed but micrometer adjustments are not essential. In contrast, autocollimators equipped with micrometers offer fine-tuning capabilities, allowing for more intricate adjustments in alignment and measurement processes, catering to specialized applications requiring enhanced precision and control in optical and mechanical systems.</p></p>
