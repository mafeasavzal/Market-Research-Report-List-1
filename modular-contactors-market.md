<p><p>The modular contactors market can be categorized into high voltage, medium voltage, and low voltage segments based on the voltage levels they manage. High voltage contactors operate at voltages above 1 kV, typically employed in large industrial applications and power generation. Medium voltage contactors, operating between 1 kV and 35 kV, are used in substation systems and manufacturing facilities. Low voltage contactors, under 1 kV, are commonly found in residential and commercial applications for controlling motors and lighting systems.</p></p>
