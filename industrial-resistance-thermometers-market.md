<p><ul><li>2 Wire</li><li>3 Wire</li><li>4 Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial resistance thermometers, commonly known as resistance temperature detectors (RTDs), are available in three primary configurations: 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire.</p><p>The 2-wire method is the simplest and most cost-effective, but it is prone to errors due to lead resistance. The 3-wire configuration enhances accuracy by compensating for some lead resistance, making it suitable for most industrial applications. The 4-wire system provides the highest precision by completely eliminating lead resistance effects, making it ideal for critical temperature measurements in advanced applications.</p></p>
