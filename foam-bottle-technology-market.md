<p><p>Foam Bottle Technology encompasses various manufacturing techniques for producing foam bottles, each suited for specific applications. Extrusion Moulding Technology involves shaping molten plastic through a die, ideal for continuous production of hollow structures. Blow Moulding Technology uses air to inflate heated plastic within a mold, creating lightweight, durable bottles. Other methods may include Injection Moulding or compression techniques, each offering unique advantages in terms of design flexibility, material efficiency, and production scalability, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
