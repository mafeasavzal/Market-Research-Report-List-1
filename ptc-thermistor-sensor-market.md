<p><p>The PTC thermistor sensor market comprises two main types: CPTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) and PPTC (Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient) devices. CPTCs are used for temperature sensing and control, exhibiting an increase in resistance with rising temperature, making them ideal for applications in heating elements and temperature monitoring. In contrast, PPTCs serve as resettable fuses, providing overcurrent protection by increasing resistance drastically when excessive current flows. Both types play vital roles in various industries, ensuring safety and efficiency in electronic devices.</p></p>
