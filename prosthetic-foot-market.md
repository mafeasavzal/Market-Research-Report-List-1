<p><ul><li>Mechanical Foot</li><li>Microprocessor Foot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prosthetic foot market is primarily divided into two main types: mechanical feet and microprocessor feet. Mechanical feet are simple, durable, and cost-effective solutions, relying on springs and hinges for motion during ambulation. They provide basic functionalities and are ideal for users seeking reliability. In contrast, microprocessor feet incorporate advanced technology to adapt to varying terrains and walking speeds, offering enhanced mobility and comfort. They feature sensors and algorithms for real-time adjustments, catering to users with specific mobility needs.</p></p>
