<p><p>The bulk material air slide market consists of various types of conveying systems for handling powders and granular materials. Dilute phase conveyors utilize high-velocity air to transport materials over long distances with minimum pressure, making them suitable for lighter products. Dense phase conveyors, on the other hand, move materials in a slower, denser flow, reducing wear and energy consumption, ideal for heavier or fragile materials. Semi-dense phase conveyors offer a balance between the two, allowing for versatility in material handling.</p></p>
