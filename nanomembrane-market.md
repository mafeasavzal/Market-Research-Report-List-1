<p><p>The nanomembrane market is categorized into three main types: polymeric membranes, hybrid membranes, and inorganic membranes. Polymeric membranes are versatile and cost-effective, suitable for filtration and separation processes. Hybrid membranes combine the advantages of both polymeric and inorganic materials, enhancing performance and stability. Inorganic membranes offer high thermal and chemical resistance, making them ideal for demanding applications. Each type serves distinct industries, including water treatment, gas separation, and biotechnology, driving innovation and efficiency in separation technologies.</p></p>
