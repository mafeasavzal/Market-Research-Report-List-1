<p><strong>The Vehicle LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Hybrid Solid-state Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle LiDAR market comprises three main types: Mechanical LiDAR, Solid State LiDAR, and Hybrid Solid-State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating components to capture detailed 3D images, offering high accuracy but more mechanical wear. Solid State LiDAR is compact and robust, employing fixed sensors for enhanced reliability and lower costs, though with reduced range. Hybrid Solid-State LiDAR combines features of both, utilizing rotating and solid-state elements to balance performance, cost, and durability, making it suitable for various automotive applications.</p></p>
