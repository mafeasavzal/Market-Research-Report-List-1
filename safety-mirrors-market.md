<p><p>The safety mirrors market comprises three main types: convex safety mirrors, flat safety mirrors, and dome safety mirrors. Flat safety mirrors offer a true reflection, commonly used for security and surveillance. Dome mirrors, featuring a hemispherical design, provide a 360-degree view, making them effective in covering large areas, particularly in parking lots and high-traffic zones. Each type serves distinct safety and security needs.</p></p>
