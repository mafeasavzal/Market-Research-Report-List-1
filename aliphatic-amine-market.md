<p><ul><li>Primary Aliphatic Amine</li><li>Secondary Aliphatic Amine</li><li>Tertiary Aliphatic Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aliphatic amine market is classified into three main types: primary, secondary, and tertiary aliphatic amines. Primary aliphatic amines consist of one alkyl group attached to the nitrogen, leading to high reactivity and versatility in applications such as agrochemicals and pharmaceuticals. Secondary aliphatic amines, with two alkyl groups, are utilized in surfactants and anti-corrosion agents. Tertiary aliphatic amines, having three alkyl groups, are common in catalysts and polymer production. Each type plays a crucial role in various industrial applications due to its unique chemical properties.</p></p>
