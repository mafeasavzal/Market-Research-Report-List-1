<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Vehicle Barrier Gate market features two primary types: straight and crank barriers. Straight barriers consist of a horizontal arm that opens and closes straight across the entry, providing a straightforward and efficient solution for controlling vehicle access. In contrast, crank barriers utilize a pivoting mechanism, often allowing for more space-efficient operation in areas where height restrictions exist. Both types enhance security and traffic management in various applications, such as parking facilities, toll booths, and secure entrances.</p></p>
