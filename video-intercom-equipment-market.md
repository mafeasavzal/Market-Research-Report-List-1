<p><p>The Video Intercom Equipment Market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and IP. Analog systems utilize traditional coaxial cables to transmit audio and video signals, offering simplicity and reliability, but with limited features and lower image quality. In contrast, IP (Internet Protocol) systems leverage internet connectivity, allowing for higher resolution video, remote access via smartphones, and integration with smart home technologies. The IP type is increasingly favored for its advanced capabilities and scalability, catering to modern security needs.</p></p>
