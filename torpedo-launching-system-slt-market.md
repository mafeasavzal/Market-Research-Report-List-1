Underwater tubes are integrated into submarines and allow for stealthy, submerged launches. In contrast, deck-mounted units are used on surface ships, facilitating rapid deployment of torpedoes while exposed to the ocean surface. Both systems are essential for modern naval warfare, enhancing a vessel's combat capabilities by providing versatile and effective torpedo launching options across various maritime scenarios.</p></p>
