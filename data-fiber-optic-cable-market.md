<p><strong>The Data Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data fiber optic cable market is primarily divided into two types: single mode and multimode. Single mode fiber cables feature a smaller core diameter, enabling the transmission of light over longer distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for long-haul communication. In contrast, multimode fiber cables have a larger core, allowing multiple light modes to travel simultaneously, which is suitable for shorter distances and higher bandwidth within buildings. Each type serves distinct applications based on distance and capacity requirements.</p></p>
