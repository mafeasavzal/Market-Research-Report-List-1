<p><p>The hem adhesive market is primarily categorized into two types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready to use and cure upon exposure to environmental conditions, offering convenience and ease of application. In contrast, two-component adhesives require a mixing process before application, leading to enhanced bonding strength and durability. Both types are widely used in automotive, construction, and manufacturing sectors for bonding materials such as metals, plastics, and fabrics, each catering to specific application needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
