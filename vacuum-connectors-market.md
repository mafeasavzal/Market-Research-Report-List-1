<p><p>The vacuum connectors market is categorized into three main types based on pressure levels: High Vacuum (HV), Ultra High Vacuum (UHV), and Extreme High Vacuum (XHV). High Vacuum typically operates between 10^-3 to 10^-7 Torr, suitable for many industrial applications. UHV ranges from 10^-7 to 10^-12 Torr, used in scientific research and semiconductor manufacturing. XHV operates below 10^-12 Torr and is essential for fundamental physics experiments and advanced materials research. Each type requires specialized connectors to maintain vacuum integrity.</p></p>
